how to play
drag the block from the bottom center onto the grid. the goal is to place it beside another brick of the same number. the smaller block on the bottom left is a next block hint. once you use the two blocks, it will be the opponents turn.
winning
points are earned when you make matches. the larger the number matched, the more points that you get. the game is over when the grid is full, and there are no more possible moves. the player with the most points wins.
